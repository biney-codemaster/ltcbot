NESTOO - MIDDLEMAN — SERVER RULES

Welcome to Nestoo. This server provides a crypto escrow service (LTC, BTC, ETH, SOL) to help buyers and sellers complete deals safely. By joining and using this server, you agree to follow these rules at all times.

────────────────────────────────────────
1. GENERAL CONDUCT
────────────────────────────────────────
• Treat all members and staff with respect. Harassment, hate speech, threats, discrimination, or targeted abuse will not be tolerated.
• Do not spam, flood channels, advertise without permission, or disrupt the server.
• Do not share illegal content, malware, phishing links, or anything that violates Discord’s Terms of Service or Community Guidelines.
• English is the primary language for support and deal communication unless staff say otherwise.

────────────────────────────────────────
2. ESCROW & DEAL RULES
────────────────────────────────────────
• Use the official deal flow only. Start deals through the designated panel and follow the bot instructions.
• Choose your role carefully. Customer pays into escrow. Seller delivers and receives the payout. Both sides must confirm roles before payment.
• Send the exact crypto amount shown. Underpayments or overpayments may not be refunded automatically. Always double-check the address and amount.
• Do not send funds outside the escrow address provided in your deal channel unless staff instruct you to.
• Do not bypass escrow, rush a release, or pressure the other party before delivery is complete.
• Cancellation is only available before a payment address is generated, through the official process in the deal channel.
• Staff decisions are final in disputes, refunds, releases, or deal cancellations when mediation is required.

────────────────────────────────────────
3. PAYMENTS & FEES
────────────────────────────────────────
• Nestoo charges 0 service fees. Only network fees apply.
• The Customer is responsible for sending the correct amount to the correct address.
• Once funds are confirmed on-chain and held in escrow, the deal follows the agreed terms and bot workflow.
• Nestoo is not responsible for user error (wrong address, wrong amount, wrong network, or third-party wallet issues).

────────────────────────────────────────
4. DELIVERY, RELEASE & REVIEWS
────────────────────────────────────────
• The Seller must deliver the agreed product or service as described in the deal.
• The Customer should only release funds after confirming receipt.
• After payout, the Customer may leave a review. Reviews must be honest and must not contain harassment, doxxing, or false accusations.
• Use /anonymous before submitting a review if you want to appear anonymous in public logs and review channels.

────────────────────────────────────────
5. STAFF & SUPPORT
────────────────────────────────────────
• Staff mediate deals; they do not guarantee outcomes. They may assist with disputes, refunds, releases, or channel management.
• Use the Staff button in your deal channel if you need help. Abuse of staff pings may result in restrictions.
• Do not impersonate staff, moderators, or the Nestoo bot.
• Staff may warn, mute, cancel deals, close channels, or remove members from the server.

────────────────────────────────────────
6. PRIVACY & SECURITY
────────────────────────────────────────
• Do not share private information unnecessarily.
• Never share your wallet seed phrase, private keys, or passwords with anyone — including staff.
• Screenshots, transcripts, and transaction IDs may be used for dispute resolution and record-keeping.
• Do not attempt to exploit, reverse-engineer, or abuse the bot or escrow system.

────────────────────────────────────────
7. PROHIBITED ACTIVITY
────────────────────────────────────────
• Scamming, fraud, or misrepresenting a product or payment
• Money laundering or illegal transactions
• Fake or duplicate deals to manipulate reviews or statistics
• Stealing funds, hijacking deals, or interfering with another user’s transaction
• Encouraging trades outside escrow

Violations may result in immediate removal and a permanent ban.

────────────────────────────────────────
8. LIABILITY
────────────────────────────────────────
• Nestoo is a technical escrow middleman, not a legal party to your agreement.
• We do not guarantee the quality, legality, or delivery of any product or service.
• Users participate at their own risk and must verify who they trade with.
• The team may refuse service, cancel deals, or restrict access at any time.

────────────────────────────────────────
9. CHANGES
────────────────────────────────────────
These rules may be updated at any time. Continued use of the server means you accept the revised rules.

────────────────────────────────────────
10. ACKNOWLEDGEMENT
────────────────────────────────────────
By using Nestoo, you confirm that you have read these rules, will follow the official escrow process, accept staff decisions in disputes, and understand that blockchain transactions are irreversible.

Need help? Read the How to use channel, then use the Staff button in your deal if something goes wrong.

Thank you for helping keep Nestoo safe and professional.
